Palmistry (Hast Rekha)

Explore the ancient art of Palmistry with our skilled practitioners. Your hands tell a unique story, and our experts decipher the lines and mounts, unveiling aspects of your past, present, and future. 

Receive personalized guidance on relationships, career, and life path based on the wisdom held in the lines of your hand. Palmistry at Pal Balaji Jyotish Sansthan is a journey of self-discovery, providing you with valuable insights to make informed decisions and navigate your life with purpose.
